“Small steps, big results.”

Warming Up

We warm up the body from top to bottom. Starting from the neck, shoulders, arms (elbows, wrists), back, hips, legs (knees, ankles)

1st Exercise

The width of the arms should be so large that when bent, the elbows reach a right angle
3 times as much as you can.

2nd Exercise

With your back straight but not leaning, explode the dumbbells above your head, bending your elbows to form a right angle 3x10-12 with 1 or 2 kg

3th Exercise

Lie on your stomach, raise your torso, elbows, and wrists as high as possible, hold for a second, and smoothly return to the starting position 3x10-12

4th Exercise

Straighten back, then do a reverse lunge, resisting the pelvis with your hands and vice versa

3x12

5th Exercise

You hold your wrists without a bend in them and perform the movement smoothly

3x10 12 with 1/2 kg in hand

6th Exercise

Back straight, knees slightly bent and elbows high without moving, only


3x10 12 with 1/2kg
