Warming Up

We warm up the body from top to bottom. Starting from the neck, shoulders, arms (elbows, wrists), back, hips, legs (knees, ankles)

1st Exercise

Squat as slowly as possible . If you have severe knee pain, stop. You will do 4 sets with as many repetitions as possible according to your own feeling, no more than 10.

2nd Exercise

Sitting on a chair, back straight and your knee slightly raised, extend and hold, doing it slowly and without touching the foot to the ground.
4x10 on each leg

3th Exercise

Keep your supporting leg slightly bent and your back straight, go down as far as possible without bending your knee more than indicated, and stand up 4x10 on each side.

4th Exercise

The important thing here is that the support is on the heels and the feet are shoulder-width apart

3x12

5th Exercise

Lean forward towards the wall until you feel a slight stretch in your calf, then begin to lift yourself up on your toes as high as possible
4x10-15
